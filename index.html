The problem

  • ⚠️ Static documents — Current tools store documents but do not monitor them.

DocuStream turns documentation into a living system: it analyzes real usage, detects obsolescence, identifies semantic relationships and generates actionable insights for technical and business teams.

🔍 Usage analytics

Detects what is consulted, when, and by whom.

🧠 Semantic graph
